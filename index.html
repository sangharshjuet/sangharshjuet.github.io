What you need to know

Sangharsh is an ultimate platform for athletes to showcase their talent and it is organised in the true spirit of sportsmanship and the fiesta begins on the 24th of March and keeps the adrenaline kicking till the 26th of March 2K17 so join us for a wonderful rondezvous of zest,ethos and sportsmanship.

Important Dates:

Registration Dates: 9th of Jan 2017 to 22nd of March 2017.

Opening Ceremony: 23rd of March 2017.

Closing Ceremony: 26th of March 2017.

A word from the JYC Chairman Prof. P.K. Singh

JUET, Guna has always strived to produce professionals that excel not only in the field of science n technology, but can also stand their ground when it comes to the field of sports. Sangharsh is one huge step that we take towards that very goal- one of many. I wish our participating students the very best in their endeavours, and hope this battle for glory brings them just that.

A word from the Sports Incharge Mr. Krishna Murari

To be trained in well-equipped laboratories, and the in the comfort of air conditioned lecture halls, and to be tempered in the heat and dust of sports, where teamwork and sportsmanship are grilled in one’s psyche by equal parts victory and defeat- are both a quota that JUET is proud to meet. We at JUET take pride as hosts to Sangharsh’17, an outstanding effort to realise the latter.
